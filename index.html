Projects

Kidlat: Lightning-Fast EV Charging Finders

Co-Developers: Alisha Chua, Javier Del Rosario, Niccolo Jimenez, Stefano Santos and Philipp Suarez

Project Timeline: May 2024 - July 2024

Kidlat is an MVP mobile app developed for the ITISDEV course, designed to help users find electric vehicle (EV) charging stations across the Philippines. It features interactive maps, real-time charger availability, pricing details, and user reviews. The app, built with Flutter, ensures a seamless user experience with intuitive navigation and real-time data. Prototyped in Figma and managed via GitHub, Kidlat addresses the needs of EV users, businesses with EV fleets, and travelers by offering route planning and notifications for charging station updates.

Ticketnet

Co-Designers: Alisha Chua and Nina Sorongon

Project Timeline: June 2024 - July 2024

TicketNet, an online ticketing platform in the Philippines, faced challenges with its cumbersome checkout process and difficulties in keeping users updated on announcements, promotions, and event details. To address these issues, we redesigned the website to streamline the ticket purchasing process, making it quicker and more efficient. Additionally, we improved the accessibility of updates and information, and ensured the site is fully optimized for mobile devices. This redesign enhances user experience by providing a more intuitive and responsive platform for purchasing tickets and staying informed about events.

CoFeed

Co-Developers: Miguel Caparos, Azeliah Martinez and Keisha Villanueva

Project Timeline: January 2024 - April 2024

Cofeed is a web platform designed for students to find and review coffee shops and study spaces. The platform allows users to browse locations, write and read reviews, create and manage profiles, and search for optimal study spots, fostering a community of shared experiences. Lashlux Studio Database

Co-Developers: Miguel Caparos, Azeliah Martinez and Keisha Villanueva

Project Timeline: October 2023 - November 2023

The LashLux Studio database application is a comprehensive system designed to manage the database of an eyelash beauty salon. The application manages essential functions such as recording and updating services offered, tracking customer and employee information, scheduling and managing appointments, and maintaining a loyalty program. It includes features for adding new services, updating service details, managing customer preferences, scheduling appointments, and tracking appointment status and retouches. Additionally, the system calculates and manages customer loyalty points, allowing customers to redeem rewards. Employee performance is monitored through metrics like customers served, appointment attendance, and revenue generated.
